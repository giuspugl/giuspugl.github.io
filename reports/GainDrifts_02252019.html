I think i've figured out why the regression in this case doesn't work. The reason is due to the fact that within the 5 minutes we regress the TOD of detB (which encodes a monopole + drift signal) against the template which is just a constant signal (around 2.7 K) . The mitigation therefore tries to fit for the extra fluctuation due to the drift and as a net result the estimation is biased . These 5 minutes bieases produce an overall effect into maps , who present stripes features together with larger residual after mitigation.

As expected, the mitigation works perfectly . The oscillation observed every 10 min is due to the spinperiod scanning the dipole signal, (~1000 times smaller than monopole)
