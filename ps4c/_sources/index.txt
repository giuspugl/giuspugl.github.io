PS4C
====

Point Source ForeCast PS4C is a package aimed at forecasting the contribution of detected and undetected extra-galactic radio sources to Cosmic Microwave Background measurements. Both intensity and polarized emissions are considered and the forecast deliver the level of contamination up to the angular power spectrum level. Download the code from `<https://gitlab.com/giuse.puglisi/PS4C>`_.
